About us

If you are looking for a perfect holiday home for your pet, while you enjoy your holiday, then you need look no further. Towsers luxury boarding Kennels in North Devon are quite simply 'the best', with every luxury and amenity to make sure your dog has the most enjoyable and memorable holiday a dog could wish for! We have 3½ acres where we can accommodate 42 dogs at a time within the 5 secure paddocks for hours of endless and safe play time. We give IS THIS CORRECT? two structured walks per day around the fields and in between we let your dog play with others of a like mind in the paddocks – always under supervision. http://www.towserskennels.co.uk/kennels_devon_facilities.html http://faithfulfriendskennels.co.uk/ If your lifestyle takes you away from the home - perhaps you work long hours or travel around a lot - then you could benefit from our doggy day care services! We can take the worry off your shoulders as you’ll know your dog won’t be spending long amounts of time alone. Our friendly staff will make sure your dog is well cared for, fed tasty meals and given special treats, if allowed, to keep them happy and healthy during their day at Green Lane Farm. Whether it’s a long term, regular arrangement or a one-off day here and there, we are more than happy to step in, and you can rest in the knowledge that your dog has been thoroughly looked after by our experienced and caring staff. We’ll guarantee they’ll have a great time too!
